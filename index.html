Poll results for FFF#309

Friday Facts #309 discusses several controversial opinions different members of the dev team have on certain game mechanics. I hastily made some polls and people have, rightly so, pointed out that the phrasing of some of the questions is really confusing. I took the tiles of each section verbatim to make sure that it was clear which proposal you're voting for but didn't realise that having things phrased negatively would muddy the waters. So I suggest you take these results with a heaping of salt.

Since the API for the polls site doesn't support CORS I need to use a reverse proxy (CORS Anywhere). A side effect of this is that the site can bump into the ratelimit so you may be viewing older data.
